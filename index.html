About Me

In my life, I aim to leave a lasting impact on the world. My ambition is to build ventures that create meaningful, long-term change. I focus on projects that redefine industries, unlock the potential of technology, and set new standards for what’s possible.

I’m currently an MBA Candidate at the University of Louisiana while serving full-time as Chief of Staff at Crowdsurf, an investment platform funding creators, solopreneurs, and founders. Our mission is to rethink how individuals raise capital by replacing institutional gatekeepers with community-driven investing.

Having been an NCAA D1 football player at both Texas A&M and the University of Louisiana, I learned how to lead under pressure, stay disciplined through adversity, and commit fully to a shared vision.

My Work

I currently serve as Chief of Staff at Crowdsurf, where we fund ambitious individuals—creators, solopreneurs, and founders—by enabling them to raise capital directly from their communities. We’ve helped dozens of people launch their dreams by replacing gatekeepers with grassroots investment.
